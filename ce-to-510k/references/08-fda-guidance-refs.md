Verbatim source list. Cite by full title and issue date in every submission document.

## Indications for Use vs Intended Use

FDA distinguishes:

- **Intended Use** = general purpose of the device
- **Indications for Use** = specific disease/condition + patient population

Form 3881 is for **Indications for Use**. Keep it concise (often 4-8 sentences). Include precautions and special populations if applicable.
